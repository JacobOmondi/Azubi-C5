ROCK WORLD

Rock world is about transforming the lifes of individuals and childrens living in the low income residential
areas of the Kenyan Eastlands. Rock world was established in the year 2012.

Rock world was established by Jacob Omondi with the vision of making the world a better place to live in.

MISSION

Our mission is to empower every person living in the low income residential areas in Kenya and enable them
to achieve more.
